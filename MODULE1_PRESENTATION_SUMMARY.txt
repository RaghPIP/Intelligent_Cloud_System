## üîë MOST IMPORTANT FEATURES

### Network Characteristics That Predict Attacks:

```
1. Protocol Type (14.68%)
   ‚îú‚îÄ TCP vs UDP vs ICMP
   ‚îú‚îÄ Different protocols = different attack vectors
   ‚îî‚îÄ Example: TCP floods vs ICMP bursts

2. Guest Login Status (10.89%)
   ‚îú‚îÄ Whether connection used guest login
   ‚îú‚îÄ Indicator of unauthorized access attempts
   ‚îî‚îÄ Strong predictor of attacks

3. Destination Host Count (8.70%)
   ‚îú‚îÄ How many times destination is contacted
   ‚îú‚îÄ Repeated connections indicate scanning/probing
   ‚îî‚îÄ Normal users ‚Üí few hosts, Attackers ‚Üí many hosts

4. Service Count Ratio (7.76%)
   ‚îú‚îÄ Distribution of services contacted
   ‚îú‚îÄ Attackers target specific services
   ‚îî‚îÄ Normal traffic ‚Üí diverse services

5. Byte Ratio (6.29%)
   ‚îú‚îÄ Upload vs download ratio
   ‚îú‚îÄ Attacks have unusual ratios
   ‚îî‚îÄ Example: Exfiltration has high upload ratio
```
